Local Government entry points

The six states and the Northern Territory have established one further level of government. Local governments (also known as city, shire, town or municipal councils) handle community needs like waste collection, public recreation facilities and town planning.

The Australian Local Government Association is the national voice of local government, representing 560 councils across the country. State local government directories provide links to local council sites:
